TOM NEVAT WEBSITE - HOW TO USE

1. Open index.html to preview the site on your computer.
2. Main editing file: js/data.js
3. Images are inside the images folder.
4. When uploading to GitHub, upload ALL files and folders in this folder:
   index.html, about.html, contact.html, css, js, images, etc.
5. Important: index.html must be at the top level of the GitHub repository, not inside another folder.
